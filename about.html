About Me

Passionate developer, creating innovative solutions for a sustainable future.

Hi, I'm JD Erasmus, a passionate and motivated software developer with a strong desire to create user-friendly applications and innovate in the field of technology. I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science: Software Engineering, equipping me with a solid foundation in software development principles and best practices

My passion for software development extends beyond the technical aspects. I'm also driven by the potential of technology to create positive change in the world. I'm particularly interested in AI, data analysis, and their applications in environmental sustainability.

My diverse skillset includes:

  1. Programming languages: Java, HTML, CSS, Python
  2. Design tools: Figma
  3. Additional skills: Photography

As an aspiring full-stack software engineer, I'm eager to learn and grow. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to improve my skills and contribute to meaningful projects. I believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, and I'm excited to work with talented individuals to build innovative solutions.

Beyond technology, I'm also passionate about photography. This creative outlet allows me to explore the world around me and capture its beauty in a unique way. I believe that the skills developed through photography, such as composition and attention to detail, translate well into my software development work.

I'm excited to see what the future holds and to continue learning, growing, and contributing to the world of technology.
