Introduction

Passcard is a digital business card solution that replaces traditional business cards with a modern, contactless system. Developed as part of a two-man team at Green Enterprise Solutions, Passcard was successfully showcased at the 2024 ITC Summit in Namibia, where over 100 users signed up during the 3-day event.

The project was built using .NET6, offering a seamless web experience for users to manage their public profiles. Passcard profiles host essential contact details such as a user's phone number, email, bio, company name, website, and LinkedIn profile. The app makes it easy to share this information without the hassle of carrying a stack of business cards.

Project Background

Passcard provides users with a printed NFC-enabled card, complete with a QR code. When the card is scanned or tapped with a smartphone, the user's profile opens instantly on the Passcard website. This technology eliminates the need for physical business cards, offering a single digital solution for networking.

Key Features

  • Contactless sharing of profiles via NFC and QR code scanning.
  • Profiles store key information: phone number, email, bio, company name, website, and LinkedIn.
  • Seamless integration of physical and digital experiences for networking.
  • User-friendly interface to manage and update profiles easily.

Implementation

Passcard was built using .NET6, leveraging modern web technologies to ensure a responsive and secure experience. The backend handles user authentication, profile management, and NFC/QR code integration. The user profiles are designed to be simple yet effective, making it easy to share details in a professional setting.

Future Enhancements

  • Integration with additional social media platforms and contact methods.
  • Expanded user customization options for profile layouts and themes.
  • Enhanced analytics to track interactions and profile visits.
  • Improved security features, including two-factor authentication for user profiles.

Conclusion

Passcard is a game-changer for professionals looking to streamline their networking efforts. With NFC technology and QR codes, it's easier than ever to share contact information. The project's success at the ITC Summit highlights its potential to revolutionize how we exchange business details.
